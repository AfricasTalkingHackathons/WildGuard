The Conservation Challenge

Africa's wildlife faces unprecedented threats, but current conservation efforts are fragmented and reactive.

Fragmented Data

Wildlife sightings and threat information scattered across NGOs, rangers, and communities with no unified system.

Slow Response

By the time threats are reported, damage is done. Patrols waste resources without predictive insights.

Low Community Engagement

Local communities lack safe, rewarding channels to contribute despite having the best ground intelligence.

Poaching costs Africa billions annually in lost eco-tourism
70% mobile penetration in rural Africa represents untapped conservation potential

The WildGuard Solution

A mobile-first wildlife database that transforms community reports into actionable conservation intelligence.

Collect Reports

Communities and rangers log poaching incidents, habitat threats, and wildlife sightings via USSD, SMS, or Voice - no internet required.

Build Database

Each verified report updates a central record of animal movements, threats, and habitat conditions.

Provide Intelligence

Live maps and historical trends help rangers plan smarter patrols and interventions.

Incentivize Participation

Verified reports earn airtime rewards and recognition badges, building a trusted local network.
